The Hidden Risks in Your Images

When you snap a photo on your smartphone or generate an image using AI tools, a significant amount of data is embedded directly into the file. This includes:

  • EXIF Data: The exact time, date, camera model, and precise GPS location where the photo was taken.
  • C2PA Content Credentials: Digital signatures linking an image to specific AI models, editing software, or content provenance standards.
  • XMP Tags: Adobe’s extensible metadata that records your editing history and generative fill prompts.

Sharing an unscrubbed photo on a forum, a dating app, or a public classifieds site can accidentally reveal your home address or daily routines. Even on social media, where platforms strip some EXIF data, embedded C2PA credentials can trigger unwanted "Made with AI" labels.
